Privacy

At FileConverter, your privacy is extremely important to us. We are committed to ensuring that your data remains secure and is handled with full transparency. All files uploaded to our platform are processed temporarily for the sole purpose of conversion. We do not store, view, or share your files beyond what is necessary to complete the requested task.

Once the conversion process is completed, your files are automatically deleted from our system within a short period of time. We do not maintain permanent backups of any uploaded content. Our goal is to minimize data retention and maximize user privacy at every step.

We do not require users to create accounts or provide personal information to use our service. This ensures a simple and private experience for everyone. By using FileConverter, you agree to this privacy policy, which may be updated in the future to reflect improvements in security and service.
