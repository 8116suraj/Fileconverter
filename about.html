About Us

FileConverter is a fast, simple, and secure tool designed to help you convert files effortlessly. Whether you're working with images, documents, or other formats, our goal is to make the process smooth and accessible for everyone.

We focus on speed, simplicity, and privacy. Your files are processed instantly and are never stored permanently on our servers.

Built with a minimal and modern approach, FileConverter aims to provide a clean experience without unnecessary complexity.
