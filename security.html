Security

Security is a core priority at FileConverter. We implement multiple layers of protection to ensure that your files and interactions remain safe throughout the entire process.

All file transfers are handled in a controlled environment, and we ensure that data is processed securely. We do not expose your files to third parties unless absolutely necessary for functionality.

Files are stored only temporarily during processing and are automatically deleted after completion. This significantly reduces the risk of unauthorized access or data leakage.

We continuously improve our systems to align with modern security practices. However, users are advised not to upload highly sensitive or confidential files as an extra precaution.
